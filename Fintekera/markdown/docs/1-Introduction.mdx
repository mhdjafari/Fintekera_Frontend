## Introduction

Welcome to our API documentation!

Our product is designed to efficiently and securely verify applicant income and ability-to-pay, all without the need for sensitive, personally identifiable information (PII).

Powered by cutting-edge AI technology, our income verification tool transcends traditional methods, delivering unmatched accuracy and cost-effectiveness across employment types and job titles. With our solution, you can effortlessly verify applicants' income without the inconvenience of accessing their bank accounts or paychecks.
